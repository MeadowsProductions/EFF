EFF - Nine Spin Off






So, how do you play?

You start off with a 3x3 grid that has random letters from A-I and there is an extra slot at the bottom, you can click around these grid slots and need to get them in alphabetic order (A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H, I).

Its kinda difficult to explain without a visual so just go ahead and start to see.
